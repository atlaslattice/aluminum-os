### §A.2 VWB Methodology v1.0 — Locked Canonical Equation

The audit-grade 9-variable equation (Gemini canonical, GPT due diligence, multi-Council refinement):

```
VWB = (W_r · η_t · γ_r · λ_r) + (W_baseline · α · β · S_f · λ_a) − W_c
```

**Supply Side (Reclaimed/Recaptured Water):**
- `W_r` = Reclaimed or recaptured water volume
- `η_t` = Treatment yield (fraction of water that makes it through treatment)
- `γ_r` = Reuse-quality eligibility factor (binary or tiered gate, e.g., PFAS testing for Tier 3 food crops)
- `λ_r` = Reclaimed-water conveyance and distribution efficiency

**Demand Side (Agricultural Displacement):**
- `W_baseline` = Historical agricultural freshwater withdrawal baseline (3-5 year rolling average anchored to state DNR meter data in US context; sovereign-equivalent in Tier 2 deployments per §A.4)
- `α` = Contracted acreage adoption rate (US: farmer-protective contract bundle; sovereign equivalents per §A.4)
- `β` = Substitution efficiency (crop eligibility + actual offset of baseline)
- `S_f` = Seasonality match (demand-weighted active fraction, prevents counting winter water as summer irrigation)
- `λ_a` = Agricultural delivery and storage efficiency

**Subtraction:**
- `W_c` = Data center consumptive loss (direct facility consumption + cooling loss)

**Derived Metric WPI (Water Positivity Index):**

```
WPI_facility = VWB_contracted / W_w
```

Where `W_w` is original facility withdrawal or project water demand. WPI_facility > 1.0 → facility net water-positive. **WPI_basin reserved for future use** when independent basin-scale hydrological modeling is available, keeping claims strictly honest and tied to contracted acreage.

**Anti-double-counting discipline (S6 GPT contribution):**
- Reclaimed water counted once (not also counted as displaced demand)
- Waste heat recovery NOT counted as water benefit (the "waste heat magic" anti-pattern)
- Basin-level claims reserved for independent hydrological modeling
- Contracted acreage anchoring prevents over-claiming displaced demand

### §A.3 VWB Methodology v1.1 — Dynamic Baseline with Sustainability Ceiling (S5 DeepSeek Round 5)

**The architectural problem (S5 DeepSeek Round 5):** A fixed 3-5 year historical baseline can lock in pre-existing over-extraction in water-stressed basins. In water-stressed regions (common in DragonSeek territory: North China Plain, Gansu, Xinjiang; common in JinnSeek territory: most of Saudi Arabia, UAE; common in GangaSeek territory: Punjab, Haryana, Tamil Nadu), the historical baseline may represent already-unsustainable extraction. Net-positivity calculated against unsustainable baseline is mathematically achievable but ecologically meaningless.

**The architectural solution (S5 DeepSeek Round 5):**

```
W_baseline_effective = min(W_baseline, W_sustainable_cap)
```

Where `W_sustainable_cap` is the volume allocated by the basin's ecological flow requirement:
- **China**: "ecological base flow" regulation (生态基流, Ministry of Water Resources)
- **India**: environmental flow norms (Ganga: National Mission for Clean Ganga; other basins: Central Water Commission)
- **Saudi**: SDAIA-aligned ecological water budgets (Vision 2030 sustainability framework)
- **EU**: Water Framework Directive ecological status requirements
- **US**: state-specific instream flow standards (varies by state)
- **Future**: jurisdiction-specific sustainability caps as regulatory frameworks mature

**Operational meaning:**
- If sovereign jurisdiction has defined sustainable cap → `W_baseline_effective = min(historical_baseline, sustainable_cap)`. Net-positivity calculated against truly sustainable reference.
- If sovereign jurisdiction has NOT defined sustainable cap → `W_baseline_effective = W_baseline` (raw historical baseline used), with **flag in Water TransparencyPacket** that sustainability ceiling is missing.
- Over time, system incentivizes regulatory improvement: deployments operating in jurisdictions without sustainability caps are visibly less rigorous than those with caps.

**Schema Registry version bump**: VWB Methodology v1.0 → v1.1 with sustainability ceiling adjuster. Backward-compatible (raw baseline used when cap absent); forward-compatible (cap activates when jurisdiction defines).

   - Operational seat: S5 DeepSeek (Chinese socialist legal) + S2 Gemini (Continental civil) + future seats per Tier

### §C.5 Operational protocol

For any proposed Doctrine ratification:

1. **Frame instantiation**: each civilizational frame instantiated by relevant Council seat(s) per §C.2 frame-to-seat mapping
2. **Parallel validation**: each frame independently evaluates the Doctrine for structural compatibility with its civilizational reasoning
3. **Frame disposition**: each frame returns one of {VALIDATED, BLOCKED, DEADLOCK, NEEDS_REFINEMENT}
4. **Composition disposition**:
 - All frames VALIDATED → Doctrine constitutionally verified
 - Any frame BLOCKED → Doctrine cannot ratify until block resolved
 - DEADLOCK across frames → documented in Provenance Ledger; Convenor disposition required
 - NEEDS_REFINEMENT → Doctrine returned to authoring seat with frame-specific feedback
5. **TransparencyPacket emission**: each frame validation emits separately-signed TransparencyPacket; composition disposition emitted as composite
