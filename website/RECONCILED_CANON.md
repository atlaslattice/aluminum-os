### Site Expansions (E153-E156) — Kept, with E153 reassigned to Energy

The site had E153=Economic & Financial Systems, E154=Physical Compute, E155=Provenance & Identity, E156=Education & Knowledge Transfer. Energy was incorrectly assigned to E148.

**Reconciliation decision:** Energy is a legitimate civilizational substrate (cuts across 3+ Houses, condition of civilization). It replaces Economic & Financial Systems at E153, since economics is better housed in H11 (Business, Economics & Infrastructure) and H10-S7 (Economics). The remaining three site VIPs are kept.

| ID | Old (Site) | New (Reconciled) | Rationale |
|----|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| E153 | Economic & Financial Systems | **Energy & Power Systems** | Energy was misassigned to E148; it's a genuine cross-House substrate (power grids, renewables, nuclear, energy policy). Economics is better served by H10-S7 + H11. |
| E154 | Physical Compute & Infrastructure | Physical Compute & Infrastructure | Kept — compute zones, hardware diversity, edge/air-gapped, regenerative facilities are genuinely cross-House. |
| E155 | Provenance & Identity | Provenance & Identity | Kept — temporal/version/error-mode provenance + identity sovereignty are genuinely cross-House. |
| E156 | Education & Knowledge Transfer | Education & Knowledge Transfer | Kept — pedagogical systems, knowledge preservation, cross-cultural transfer are genuinely cross-House (H9 Knowledge Systems is the House home, but the substrate cuts across all Houses). |
